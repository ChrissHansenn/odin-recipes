Lemon Chicken

Our easy lemon chicken recipe shines with a sunny, lemony zing. Garlic and herbs—plus a glug of white wine—mean that these juicy, tender chicken breasts are as delicious as they are healthy.

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic (6 cloves)
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest (2 lemons)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 lemon, sliced in 6 wedges, optional
  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Pat the chicken breasts dry and place them in a 9 x 13 baking dish
  3. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, oregano, thyme, garlic powder, 1 teaspoon salt and pepper to create a thick marinade/paste. Coat the chicken breast with seasoning paste
  4. Using the same bowl mix together the white wine, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, and remaining salt. Pour over chicken
  5. If using, nestle lemon slices between the chicken. Bake for 15 minutes, baste the chicken with the pan juice, bake for another 15 minutes or until internal temp reaches 165 F
